 # Blueberry Banana Protein Smoothie

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 banana
- 1/3 cup frozen blueberries
- 1 tsp blue spirulina powder
- 1 scoop protein powder
- 1 tsp honey

## Instructions
1. Blend 1/2 cup milk, 1 banana, 1/3 cup frozen blueberries, 1 tsp blue spirulina powder, 1 scoop protein powder, and 1 tsp honey in a blender until smooth.
