 # Healthier McChicken Sandwich

## Ingredients for 6 Sandwiches
- 600g Chicken Breast (I used Tenderloin for lower calorie)
- 1 tbsp Mustard
- 1 tsp Paprika
- 1 tsp Cayenne
- 2 tsp Garlic Powder
- 1 tsp Onion Powder
- 1 tsp Salt
- 2 tsp Black Pepper
- 1 tsp Celery powder
- Corn flake crumbs, about 10g per patty
- Low calorie buns (I used Culture Keto Buns)
- Romaine Lettuce
- 7g Light Mayo (I used Miracle Whip light mayo)

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
2. In a bowl, mix together mustard, paprika, cayenne, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and celery powder.
3. Cut chicken breast into patty size and coat evenly with the spice mixture.
4. Dip each coated chicken patty in corn flake crumbs to fully cover.
5. Place chicken patties onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees Fahrenheit.
7. Toast low calorie buns in the oven or toaster if desired.
8. Assemble sandwiches by placing a cooked chicken patty on each bun, adding romaine lettuce and a spread of light mayo.

## Notes
- The calorie count for this recipe is per sandwich and will vary depending on mayo brand and bun brand.
- Proper portioning is important for accurate calorie intake. Consider learning about portion sizes or investing in a food scale for measurement.
